Mandate selection guidance

High-confidence match

  • Board and C-suite need a practical operating model
  • Adoption and workflow change are central

Partner or qualify carefully

  • Client primarily needs frontier-model research leadership
  • Role is principally a hands-on ML engineering position
  • Highly specialized regulation requires pre-existing licensed expertise
  • Client will not grant decision access or workflow visibility
  • Expectation is unlimited execution without internal owners
